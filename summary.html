Disclaimer
• The GHG footprint is expressed in CO2 equivalent emissions (CO2e). This includes CO2, CH4, N2O greenhouse gas emissions. Total GHG emissions are estimated using the IPCC Fifth Assessment Report (AR5) global warming potentials (GWP) on 100-year time scale.
• The calculation of GHG footprint uses both primary and secondary data, which may contain uncertainties and are calculated based on 12 months rolling total.
• The full lifecycle intensity has considered the gas consumption and loss for each stage, therefore it is not the sum of the intensity from each stage.
• For integrated assets, only the total Well to Loading Arm intensity is known. For these assets, the Liquefaction stage shows that WtLA total and the Upstream stage is zero.
